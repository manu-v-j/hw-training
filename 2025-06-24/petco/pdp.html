      Nulo MedalSeries Bone Broth is made by gently cooking high-quality beef bones for up to 10 hours to extract nourishing collagen protein and infuses ingredients like parsley, thyme and turmeric to make a wholesome broth. Simply pour over your dog or cat's next kibble or freeze-dried meal to add nourishment and flavor.

      • - Contains superfoods like apple cider vinegar and turmeric
      • - Naturally rich in collagen
      • - Savory flavor can entice the pickiest of pets
