Brand information

Blending preppy American casualness with refined European elegance, Bernard Gant established Gant in 1949 in New Haven, USA. Starting off with a humble selection of shirts, Gant now offers an enviable range of sophisticated premium casualwear, synonymous with effortless and understated style.
